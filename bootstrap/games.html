KRATOS

We show a brief look at two of the main antagonists you'll face in God of War Ragnarök: Freya and Thor. Because of Kratos' actions at the end of the previous game, the once-ally-turned-enemy Freya has sworn vengeance for the death of her son, Baldur.

ATREUS

Atreus is the son of Kratos in God of War and during the events of the previous game, Atreus and his father traveled throughout the Norse realms to fulfill his mother's dying wish. Faye, later revealed as Laufey, was a Frost Giant and the second wife of Kratos.

FREYA

God of War Ragnarok takes place approximately three years after God of War (2018), meaning that it has been three years since Freya swore vengeance upon Kratos for the death of her son, Baldur. Baldur was the primary antagonist of God of War (2018), and the Norse God of Light is actually the son of both Freya and Odin.
